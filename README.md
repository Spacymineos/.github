## 📦 What is this?

**Spacymineos** is a GitHub organization that holds all of my retired, shelved,
and deprecated projects. These repos are no longer actively maintained, but they
are kept public for reference, learning, or inspiration.

---

## ⚠️ Important Notice

> All repositories in this organization are **archived and read-only**.  
> - ❌ No active development  
> - ❌ No bug fixes or updates  
> - ✅ Public access & forking allowed  
> - ✅ Free to use as reference or learning material  

---

## 🛸 Why keep them?

Every project, no matter how old or broken, represents a step in the journey.
Instead of deleting them, they drift here — frozen in time, open to the world.
